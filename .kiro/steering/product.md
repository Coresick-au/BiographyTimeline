# Product Overview

Users Timeline is a collaborative digital historiography platform that transforms personal photo collections into rich, interactive timelines with narrative storytelling capabilities.

## Core Purpose

The application automatically ingests photo metadata to create chronological timelines, allows users to create rich narrative "Long Stories" for significant events, and enables collaborative timeline merging between users to visualize shared histories and relationships.

## Key Features

- **Automated Timeline Creation**: Extracts EXIF data from photos to build chronological timelines without manual date entry
- **Intelligent Event Clustering**: Groups photos by temporal and spatial proximity into meaningful events
- **Rich Storytelling**: Scrollytelling interface for creating narrative content with embedded media
- **Social Timeline Merging**: Connect with others to view shared history through "River Visualization"
- **Privacy-First Design**: Granular privacy controls with end-to-end encryption for sensitive content
- **Offline-First Architecture**: Full functionality without internet connectivity with seamless sync

## Target Users

- Individuals wanting to preserve and organize their digital memories
- Families and couples looking to create shared digital heritage
- Anyone interested in collaborative storytelling and relationship visualization
- Users who value privacy and data sovereignty in their personal archiving tools

## Value Proposition

Transform chaotic digital photo collections into coherent, navigable, and collaborative biographies that preserve both the visual record and the human stories behind significant life moments.
